DCPS.DC.GOV Website Redesign

Problem

Families need to find out information about the DC Public Schools because they are moving to the area. Recent gradutes are seeking employment with DCPS. They are having difficulty locating the information they need to make decisions for their children and career paths. In addition, the amount of information on the website is overwhelming for some. The cumbersome navigation is frustrating users.

Solution

How might we assess the current website and determine road blocks that visitors are facing? Once we determine what hierarchy and UI patterns currently being used on the website, how might we offer more efficiencies and new ways to break up the information or manage the volume of information that is overwhelming users?
